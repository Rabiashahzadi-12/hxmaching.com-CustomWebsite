Uncompromising Quality Standards

Our commitment to quality is backed by international certifications, advanced inspection equipment, and rigorous quality control processes at every stage of production.

6-Step Quality Control Process

1

 Material Verification

Incoming material inspection with material certification review and chemical composition analysis

2

 First Article Inspection

Complete dimensional inspection of first production piece with full measurement report

3

 In-Process Control

Real-time monitoring during machining with SPC data collection and analysis

4

 Final Inspection

100% dimensional verification using CMM and optical measurement systems

5

 Documentation

Complete traceability documentation including material certs, inspection reports, and process records

6

 Customer Approval

PPAP submission and customer approval process for new parts and engineering changes
